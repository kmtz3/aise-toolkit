name: session-score
description: Score a delivered session against its scorecard
---

Score the session.

1. Find the session's notes/transcript — follow the **Transcript lookup order** in `context/project-instructions.md §3`.
2. Load the matching scorecard rows from `context/score-cards.md`.
3. Score each dimension 1–5 with a one-line rationale tied to specific evidence from the notes.
4. Flag every dimension that scored **below 4** — what specifically dragged it down and what would have moved it up.
5. Give a single-paragraph overall assessment and the 1–3 concrete things to change next time.

Keep it honest — scorecards exist to diagnose, not validate. Don't score what isn't evidenced in the source.
